  2. Limit to restrict the amount of money that may be wagered within a specified period of time.
What is the difference between cooling off and self-exclusion?

A cooling-off period is when a Player wishes to take a small break from playing. This period can range from a week up to 6 months. While on a cool-off, Player can still login to his/her Account however, Player will be blocked from depositing and any promotions we have will not be sent to the Player.

Player’s Account will be activated automatically when the cooling-off period is up.

A self-exclusion is a longer period of time, 6 months to permanently, and when active, a Player can no longer log into his/her Account, and his/her Account cannot be reopened before the time limit has expired. The player will need to contact us after the limit has expired, so we can reopen the account once more.

A Player who has set a limit or exclusion may change or revoke the limit or exclusion by written notice or electronic notice given to Bet Happy.

Can I permanently close my Account?

If a Player wishes to close their account permanently then they will have to contact our Support team on {{websiteEmail}}, followed by a brief explanation as to why they want to leave Bet Happy. By doing this, Player’s Account will be closed forever and he/she will be unable to re-open it again.

Please note, that these Players will not be able to open a new account either.
